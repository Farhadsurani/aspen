What the delay status means:

LOW - This means we experience little or no delays at the Aspen airport due to weather. If delays did pop up, they would be short-lived. MEDIUM - This means cloud ceilings, visibility, or wind problems at the airport might be an issue for a portion of the day. Delays/cancellation are possible but won't last all day and night HIGH - This means either cloud ceilings/visibilities/wind are going to be beow airport minimums for a significant peroid of time. Delays and cancellations are a good possibility.
